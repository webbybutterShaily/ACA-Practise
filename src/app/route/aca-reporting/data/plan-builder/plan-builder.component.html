In the Plan Builder you will create any plan that is or will be effective during the applicable reporting year. The information that Users provide in the Plan Builder is directly related to Affordable Care Act information reporting and must be accurate. If there are multiple contribution structures for a plan, consider each one as its own unique plan which needs to be built in the Plan Builder. Example: Plan A is offered and for a subset of employees they are required to contribute $100 dollars a month if they were to enroll in self-only coverage, and for a different subset of employees the required contribution is $150 a month if they were to enroll in self-only coverage. In this scenario these would be created as two separate plans.
