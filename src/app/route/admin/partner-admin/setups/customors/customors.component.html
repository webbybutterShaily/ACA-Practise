help Check this box if the payroll data field that is being provided by the customer or on behalf of the customer is W-2 Wages instead of a Rate of Pay. Show Employee ID in Lists
help Check this box if the customer wants to see the Employee ID that is associated with Employees in each table in the Dashboard. If not checked, the Employee ID will not appear.
Show max number of hours to maintain non-full time status column
help The system default is that this box is checked. This is a column specifically located in the ‘Tracked Employees’ table in the Dashboard. The column identifies the number of hours that a tracked employee can work each week throughout the remainder of a measurement period and keep their total average hours per week under 30. To hide this column of information you would simply uncheck this box.
Monthly Measurement
help If the customer indicates that they would like to use the Monthly Measurement Method, rather than the Look-Back Measurement Method to track the full-time status of their employees, then select ‘Yes’ in this drop-down menu. *Note That the customer must provide their payroll data broken down by month for this tracking method to be accurate in the system. Failure to do so will lead to inaccurate tracking results.
